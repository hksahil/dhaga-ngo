Vision of the DHAGA SEVABHAVI SANSTHA is to provide educational material as well as enhance their skill, self-confidence, personality development and addition of useful knowledge besides regular studies. This vision of the DHAGA SEVABHAVI SANSTHA is for students who have enrolled in a school but their parents cannot afford educational material and the students have to discontinue their education. The focus of the organization is such students should not suffer academic loss and give direction towards best future.



DHAGA SEVABHAVI SANSTHA is volunteer based trust. Activities of the DHAGA-SEVABHAVI SANSTHA planned and conducted by volunteers.
